Commonly Asked Cultivation Questions

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We nurture general inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Technical queries may take up to 48 hours for detailed cultivation responses. For urgent matters, please call our support numbers directly.

Do you provide site visits and demonstrations?

Yes, we arrange site visits to our sustainable demonstration farms and product demonstrations at our green facilities. Please schedule in advance through our demo request form or by calling our office.

What information should I provide for a technical inquiry?

For technical inquiries, please provide: current energy consumption, application type (cooking/drying/heating), capacity requirements, site location, and any specific challenges you're facing. Site photos are helpful for proper cultivation planning.

How can I check subsidy eligibility for my project?

Our team assists with subsidy applications and cultivation. Please provide your project details and location, and we'll check eligibility for various government schemes and guide you through the application cultivation process.

Do you provide training for system operation and maintenance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for operators and maintenance staff. Training is included with system installation, and we also offer periodic refresher courses and advanced cultivation programs.

Still Have Cultivation Questions?

Our team of green energy cultivators is ready to help you grow your sustainable projects and answer any questions about our technologies.
