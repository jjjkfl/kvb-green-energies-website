Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We respond to general inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Technical queries may take up to 48 hours for detailed responses. For urgent matters, please call our support numbers.

Do you provide site visits and demonstrations?

Yes, we arrange site visits to existing installations and product demonstrations at our facilities. Please schedule in advance through our demo request form or by calling our office.

What information should I provide for a technical inquiry?

For technical inquiries, please provide: current fuel consumption, application type (cooking/drying/heating), capacity requirements, site location, and any specific challenges you're facing. Site photos are helpful.

How can I check subsidy eligibility for my project?

Our team assists with subsidy applications. Please provide your project details and location, and we'll check eligibility for various government schemes and guide you through the application process.

Do you provide training for system operation and maintenance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for operators and maintenance staff. Training is included with system installation, and we also offer periodic refresher courses and advanced training programs.
