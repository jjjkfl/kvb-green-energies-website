Commonly Asked Cultivation Questions

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We nurture general inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Technical queries may take up to 48 hours.

Do you provide site visits and demonstrations?

Yes, please schedule in advance through our demo request form.

What information should I provide for a technical inquiry?

Please provide current energy consumption, application type, capacity requirements, site location, and any specific challenges.

How can I check subsidy eligibility for my project?

Our team assists with subsidy applications. Provide your project details and we'll check eligibility.

Do you provide training for system operation and maintenance?

Yes, comprehensive training is included with installation, and we offer periodic refresher courses.
