Our Story

KVB Green Energies is a renewable energy and smart agriculture technology company focused on designing sustainable infrastructure for community cooking, industrial heating, and agricultural value addition.

Founded with over three decades of solar expertise, the company bridges engineering innovation with real-world applications. Our work integrates solar thermal technology, energy storage, and intelligent agricultural systems to build scalable solutions for institutions and farmers.

What began as a research initiative in solar thermal applications has now evolved into a national-scale operation, impacting thousands of lives across India while significantly reducing carbon emissions.

Our Vision

To engineer a future where clean energy and intelligent agriculture form the backbone of sustainable development, creating self-reliant communities and carbon-neutral industries.

Our Mission

To reduce fossil fuel dependence by 50% in community cooking and industrial heating, empower 1 million farmers through technology, and deliver reliable renewable solutions for large-scale impact by 2030.

Leadership
