Environmental Impact: Before vs After

Quantifying the difference KVB systems make

Firewood vs Solar Cooking: Environmental Impact

Firewood Cooking (Per 100 meals)

  • 50-100 kg of firewood consumed
  • 150-300 kg CO₂ emissions
  • Deforestation impact
  • Indoor air pollution health risks

Solar Cooking (Per 100 meals)

  • Zero fuel consumption
  • Zero operational emissions
  • No deforestation
  • Clean indoor air

LPG vs Solar Cooking: Cost & Environmental Analysis

LPG Cooking (Annual for 500 meals/day)

  • ₹650,000 - ₹800,000 fuel cost
  • 10-12 tons CO₂ emissions
  • Fossil fuel dependency
  • Price volatility risks

Solar Cooking (Annual for 500 meals/day)

  • 70-80% lower operational cost
  • Near-zero emissions
  • Energy independence
  • Price stability
