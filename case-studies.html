Project Overview

The Indian Army base in Chennai required a sustainable cooking solution for their kitchen serving 500+ personnel daily. The existing LPG-based system had high operational costs and supply chain dependencies.

Challenge

High LPG consumption, rising costs, and supply uncertainties

Solution

100 m² solar steam cooking system with hybrid backup

Results

70% LPG reduction, 10 tons CO₂ savings annually
