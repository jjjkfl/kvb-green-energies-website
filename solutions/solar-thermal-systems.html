Our Thermal Technologies

Precision engineered for every temperature requirement

Scheffler Concentrator

Fixed‑focus dish ideal for community cooking and medium‑temp process heat.

  • Temp Range: 100°C – 200°C
  • Application: Steam cooking, industrial heat
  • Efficiency: >60% thermal

Parabolic Trough

Linear concentrators for large‑scale industrial heating and power generation.

  • Temp Range: 150°C – 300°C
  • Application: Power gen, oil heating
  • Scale: Modular MW deployment
