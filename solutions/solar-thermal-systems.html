System Operation

1

Concentration

Mirrors focus sunlight onto a stationary or tracking receiver.

2

Thermal Conversion

Concentrated light converts to high-grade thermal energy in the Heat Transfer Fluid (HTF).

3

Integration

Heat is exchanged with your process application (Steam/Water/Oil) via heat exchangers.

4

Hybrid Backup

Intelligent controls switch to backup fuel only when solar energy is insufficient.
