Key Specifications

Our Hybrid Solar Tunnel Dryers are engineered for efficient, hygienic, and uniform drying of agricultural produce, with the flexibility of 24/7 operation.

Tunnel Size

10m – 50m length, fully customizable to your capacity needs.

Temperature Range

40°C – 80°C, digitally controlled with ±2°C accuracy.

Capacity

100 – 2000 kg per batch, depending on tunnel size.

Drying Time

6 – 48 hours, product‑specific programmable cycles.
